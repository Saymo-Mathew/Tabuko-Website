    Who We Are

    Founded in 2011, Tabuko Energy Network Corp. is a Philippine company engaged in sales, importation, and servicing within the power generation, electrical, and mechanical industries. Headquartered at 049 Purok 1, Brgy. Butong, City of Cabuyao, Laguna, Philippines 4025, we have built a strong reputation for delivering high-quality products and services tailored to the needs of various industries.

    We provide a wide range of products and services—from supplying essential equipment to preventive maintenance, diesel engine overhauling, and system design upgrades—so our clients receive reliable, efficient solutions for their energy and mechanical needs. Through strong partnerships and a commitment to excellence, we continue to expand our reach and deliver industry-leading solutions across the Philippines.

    Our Journey

    We started from a small office with a big vision: to bring reliable power and energy solutions to businesses across the Philippines. Over the years, we have grown from a modest operation into a trusted partner for industries that depend on uninterrupted power—investing in our team, our capabilities, and our facility. We are still improving every day: expanding our service coverage, upgrading our technical expertise, and strengthening our partnerships with leading brands. Our story is one of steady growth, commitment to quality, and a focus on long-term relationships with our clients.

    Services We Offer

    We deliver end-to-end solutions in power generation, electrical, and mechanical systems. Here is what we offer:

    Products & Equipment Genset controls, AC/DC motors, battery chargers, ATS, and quality equipment from trusted brands.
    Preventive Maintenance Routine inspections, testing, and servicing to keep your systems safe and operating at peak performance.
    Diesel Engine Overhauling Complete inspection, disassembly, and precision rebuilding following manufacturer specifications.
    System Design & Upgrades System design, electronic controls calibration, and project management for upgrades and new installations.
