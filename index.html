    Preventive Maintenance Service

    Safer systems. Higher uptime. Better efficiency.

    Our Preventive Maintenance Service keeps your electrical energy systems safe, reliable, and operating at peak performance. Through routine inspections, testing, and servicing, we detect potential issues early—reducing downtime, preventing failures, and extending equipment life.

    We maintain electrical panels, transformers, switchgear, generators, UPS systems, and power distribution networks in compliance with industry standards. Our proactive approach improves energy efficiency, enhances safety, and protects your investment.

    • Electrical panels, switchgear, and protection devices
    • Transformers, generators, and UPS systems
    • Testing, inspection, cleaning, and performance checks

    Reliable power starts with preventive care.

    Diesel Engine Overhauling Service

    Restore performance and reliability—done right.

    Our Diesel Engine Overhauling Service restores engine reliability, performance, and efficiency. Through complete inspection, disassembly, and precision rebuilding, we identify wear, correct faults, and prevent future failures.

    We overhaul diesel engines for generators, industrial equipment, and power systems following manufacturer specifications and industry standards. Our structured approach reduces fuel consumption, improves power output, and extends engine service life.

    • Detailed assessment, teardown, and parts inspection
    • Precision rebuilding and calibration to spec
    • Functional testing and performance validation

    Reliable power starts with expert overhauling.

    System Design Upgrading Service

    Modernize systems for today’s demands.

    Our System Design Upgrading Service enhances the performance, safety, and efficiency of your electrical and power systems. Through detailed assessment and engineering review, we improve existing designs to meet current demands and future growth.

    We upgrade power distribution, control systems, and energy infrastructure in accordance with industry standards and operational requirements. Our solutions reduce energy losses, improve reliability, and optimize overall system performance.

    • Engineering assessment and improvement plan
    • Control and distribution upgrades for reliability
    • Documentation and recommended preventive actions

    Smarter systems start with better design.
